# Contributing

Thanks for your interest. This repo holds the **open, free layer** of the
Embedded Reliability Data methodology: the core thinking and the event schema.

## What's welcome
- Corrections / clarifications to the methodology
- Schema field discussions and real-world variations
- Translations (the methodology is currently in Traditional Chinese)
- "I applied this to my device and here's what I learned" reports — these are
  the most valuable thing you can contribute.

## How
1. Open an issue describing the change or your use case.
2. For text/schema changes, send a PR against `main`.
3. Keep examples device-agnostic where possible — the goal is transferable
   discipline, not one specific machine.

## Scope note
The firmware templates, simulators, and full case study are **not** in this
repo by design. This repo is intentionally the methodology + schema + the
in-browser demo tools only.
